# PRELUDE

- Notes
	- 
- Beats
	- Exposition
		- Introduce [[Megara]]. She is a lone scavenger, one of countless in the unforgiving wilds of Everwinter. She is foraging for food/resources in pre-Everwinter ruins when the camera settles on her.
		- In these ruins, [[Megara]] comes blade to blade with another scavenger. She defeats them and finds they were carrying fresh meat, which is all she needs to return home to [[Anaktoria]].
		- Before returning home, though, [[Megara]] must deal with her bloodied clothing. As far as [[Anaktoria]] knows, [[Megara]] hunts wild game, not other scavengers, and the reader first learns here that [[Megara]] is hiding this fact.
